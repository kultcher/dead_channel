## Concrete Decisions

- The core fantasy is the player as the pressured voice in the ear, not as a direct commander.
- Solo-dev scope should lean into abstraction, tension, and systems interplay rather than simulated physical realism.
- The intended feel is plate-spinning panic under indirect control.

## Speculative Ideas

- The game can sit between autobattler pressure, hacking simulation, and support-role tactics.
- The overwatch concept is niche enough to feel distinctive even if inspired by Shadowrun and Invisible Inc.

## Unresolved Questions

- Exact fail state and campaign consequences remained open in the early concept phase.
- How much permanent runner loss or campaign injury the final design should tolerate.

## Terminology And Definitions

- Matrix overwatch: remote hacker support for an on-site team.
- Voice in the ear: the player's emotional and tactical role during runs.

## Mechanics And Systems Details

- Indirect control and constant triage are the backbone of the concept.
- The player should constantly choose which threat to service, delay, or accept.

## Narrative Or Tone Details

- The inspiration target is gritty contractor cyberpunk rather than superheroic netrunning power fantasy.

## Implementation Constraints

- Favor readable abstractions and manageable systems scope over literal simulation.
